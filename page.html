Babyi

Born in Italy and raised in Michigan and Brazil, Babyi has always had a passion for music and has been inspired by some of the greatest artists in the industry, such as Michael Jackson, Stromae, and Lil Wayne. Babyi moved to Los Angeles at 21 to pursue her music career, and she has been working hard to form a sound that sets her apart from other artists in the industry. As an artist, Babyi is always seeking to produce and write music that is relatable and fun.

Babyi's music is a fusion of pop, house, world, and Brazilian influences, creating a fresh and exciting listening experience for her fans. Her pop roots are evident in her catchy hooks and upbeat rhythms, while her house influence brings a danceable energy to her tracks. Babyi is a forward-thinking artist who is always looking for new and innovative ways to push the boundaries of her music.

As a person, Babyi is outgoing yet introverted, always seeking to discover more about herself. She is a believer in the power of positivity and strives to bring peace and happiness to the world through her music and charitable acts. she also has a passion for technology, hoping to one day create and produce her own apps and gadgets.
